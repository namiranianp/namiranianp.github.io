Information about me

A little about me:

I am currently a computer science major and a philosophy minor at the University of San Francisco. I was born in Yazd, Iran and moved to America around when I was 4 years old and I have spent the rest of my life growing up in the Bay Area. For as far back as I can remember, I have always had a thirst for knowledge and a desire to solve challenging problems in a unique way. It is these qualities that drew me to the fields of computer science and philosophy. I am currently feeding my hunger for knowledge by taking courses in networking and security in the hopes of creating my own operating system which focuses on the integrity of the user's files. I am also exploring and reading about the philosophies of thought, consciousness, and emotion. The area of study I am most interested in currently is the intersection of my two loves, computer science and philosophy. It is a very important topic which is becoming more and more relevant as technological advances are being made. In my opinion, the most fascinating topic to learn about and discuss is whether Artificial intelligence can ever become conscious, and whether it will ever be deserving of rights.
